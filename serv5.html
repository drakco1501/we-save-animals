  VOLENTERS :






Volunteers are the biggest support for our shelter. They visit WSA whenever they can and during times in need.
They help in cleaning the shelter, grooming our animals, treating, feeding and pampering them. During vaccination drives,
sterilization camps, awareness camps and many other programs, volunteers play a very important role.
We would love it if more volunteers join us! There is always the need for an extra helping hand.
If interested, please contact WSA.
