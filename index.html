   About the founder :



  Praveen Kumar L,
  also called Rider Praveen is an ex-gym instructor
  who gave up his job, his means of livelihood,
  all his and his familyâ€™s assets and savings
  to start We Save Animals (WSA) Welfare Trust.
  From paralysed, blind, old aged animals to accident cases,
  cruelty cases and terminally ill animals -
  he takes them all in, giving them happiness,
  food and a home in his shelter.


  It started for Praveen in 2013, when he rescued a
  kitten with a spinal injury. He nursed it at home,
  but it passed away after a week, leaving Praveen to
  wonder what he could do to help numerous animals like this,
  that suffer in silence.
